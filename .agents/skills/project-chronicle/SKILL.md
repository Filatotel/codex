# Project Chronicle

## Purpose

Use this skill to preserve the strategic history of a project.

## Why it exists

Chat history and tactical handoffs explain what happened in a session.
A project chronicle explains how the project reached its current state.

## Add entries for

- major architectural decisions
- rejected alternatives worth remembering
- important incidents or regressions
- changes in constraints
- decisions that future sessions may otherwise repeat

## Entry format

Each entry should capture:

- date
- context
- decision or incident
- rationale
- consequence
- follow-up if any

## Rules

- append, do not rewrite history casually
- prefer concrete events over abstract summaries
- record why, not just what
- avoid turning the chronicle into a daily log of trivial edits

## Good use cases

- why a framework was chosen
- why a refactor was postponed
- why a deployment path changed
- why a previous approach was abandoned
